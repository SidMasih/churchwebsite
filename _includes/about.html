About

Any journey is filled with possibilities, excitement, challenges and new beginnings around every corner. The journey of life is the same, with its many ups, downs, twists and turns. At Journey Christian Fellowship, we believe that it is in the midst of the twists and turns of life that we encounter the amazing grace, love and power of our Savior, Jesus Christ. As a church, we want to be a light and voice of Jesus Christ for those who are at a crossroads on their journey of life and walk with them as they encounter God.

  • What to Expect

    Journey Christian Fellowship is a Bible believing, Spirit-filled, welcoming church located in Walnut Creek, CA. Our goal is to lead people into a growing relationship with God no matter where they at their journey in life. We are students, professionals, parents, grandparents, children and young adults. We start our Sunday mornings with a cup of coffee and fellowship at 11:00am because we want to connect and journey together. Our worship service starts at 11:29am and is contemporary, yet entirely God-focused. If you are looking for a place to grow in your relationship with God and build a long lasting and meaningful community, we welcome you to our home, which we hope you can make it yours too.

  • What We Believe

    The core of our belief is centered on the incarnation, birth, life, death and resurrection of Jesus Christ, the Son of God. We believe in Jesus as the one who saves us from the power of sin and death, fills us with the Spirit of God to help us live in this world, heals us from distress and disease, and that He will return as promised as the King of all righteousness and peace.
